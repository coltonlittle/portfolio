Video

"Haunted by Love"

This project was created to promote, local Vancouver artist, Dominique Fricot's single, "Haunted by Love". The video goes through the many aspects of the song including: the writing process, studio production and performance.

The song itself is played throughout the entirety of the video, but is cut into three separate sections that are mixed together to create a seamless composition. The first being him playing acoustically in the park, followed by the actual mp3, and ends off with the live performance. I undertook the process of mixing the audio.

This project was filmed and edited by Colton Little and Alisa Lokshin.

Special thanks to Dominique Fricot.

Claire's Coastal Cuisine

This project is based on a fictional website called "Claire's Coastal Cuisine". The site was meant to be a resource/cooking blog focusing on west coast inspired cooking. An online personality, named Claire, would offer up her favorite recipes and once every month she would make a video blog showing viewers how to make a featured dish. The dish featured is maple salmon. I produced, and composed, the song for the video and mixed the audio.

Filmed and edited by Colton Little and Alisa Lokshin.

Apogee Promotional Video

This project is based on a fictional mountain biking company that I created called Apogee. The video is a mix of pre-shot bike footage and an interview, filmed by the team, of fictional rider Dean Stryker. The project was a great experieince because we were supplied with over a hunderd clips of biking footage to use in conjunction with our interview footage. I liked this project due to the fact that during the production of a video, you won't also be using footage that you filmed yourself. There were so many possibilities, but we were only allowed two minutes of time to fill. The audio for this project was produced and composed by myself.

Filmed and edited by Colton Little and Alisa Lokshin.
